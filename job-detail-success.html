Technical Architect

Job Description

The Technical Architect (TA) is our technical champion & advisor entrusted to be driving technical decisions through architectural reviews, technical optimizations, security reviews, and other technical discussions. The TA is also responsible for:

  • Champion best practices for our technical teams, in regards to the development process, documentation, coding, product quality, maintenance & support
  • Evolve our software architecture to incorporate business and technical requirements while ensuring high scalability, security, and performance.
  • Evaluate & drive proposed technical solutions by weighing the risks, rewards and future implications of each decision and recommending the optimal technical path forward.
  • Guide and motivate our engineering teams to continually improve their skills through mentoring, training, pair programming, code reviews and fostering an environment of excellence.
  • Develop time & effort estimates for all technical implementations and track the progress of technical teams from inception through delivery.
  • Identify bottlenecks in our applications and devise solutions to ensure the best possible performance, quality, scalability, security.

Job Requirement

  • 5+ years of experience as a software architect (or similar role) with strong hands-on experience enabling multi-threading, caching techniques, multi-platform synchronization, and microservices.
  • 8+ years of experience with architectural patterns for a large, high-scale data platform (e.g., well-designed APIs, high volume data pipelines, efficient algorithms).
  • 8+ years of excellent & recent working experience working with technologies like Objective-C, Swift, PHP, Golang, Java, C++, Javascript, NodeJS, Ruby, etc...
  • 7+ years of extensive experience building, scaling, managing platforms and APIs.
  • Excellent understanding of DevOps/CloudOps and building high-availability and high availability infrastructure.
  • Leadership that combines persuasiveness, decisiveness, and competitiveness necessary to motivate a team.
  • Experience with application build automation tools like Apache Maven, Jenkins, and Git.
  • Excellent communication skills in English.

Huge Plus If You Are/Have

  • Good understanding of Amazon AWS technologies (or Google Cloud & Azure equivalents) such as EC2, RDS, ECS, ELB, EKS, ...
  • Expert experience developing defense systems & mechanisms against cyber attacks.
  • Good understanding of relational and non-relational database systems
  • Experience evolving monolithic systems into modern microservice architectures
  • Excellent hands-on experience with PaaS, IaaS, CaaS
